My Favorite Books

"Clockwork Orange"

A Clockwork Orange is a book written in a dystopian genre by English writer Anthony Burgess. It was published in 1962. The novel is set in a near-future world with a society including a youth subculture which is famous for extreme violence. The state authority in the book tries to "reform" the protagonist Alex, a violent teenager. "The Clockwork Orange"is written in argot called "Nadsat".

This particular book made me think of the things and concepts I would never even thought I'd get interested in. "Clockwork Orange" is one of the books that make you feel fallen out of the world for a few days.

.

"American Gods"

"American Gods" is a book written by Neil Gaiman, one of my favorite authors. It was published in 2001 (strange thing I got to know about its existance only a year ago!). This novel is a mix of fantasy, Americana, different stands of ancient and modern mythology. The main charachter Shadow travels across the country with a mysterious friend called Mr.Wednesday, meeting different gods and having truly strange adventures.

Honestly, I love this book for its WEIRD and PLEASANT atmosphere, strangly awesome plot and a shocking ending. Also, there's a new TV show based on this book, which turned out not bad!
