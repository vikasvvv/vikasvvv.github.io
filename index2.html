What I Do Every Year

Every year, I set for myself a goal to read a specific number of books. Such promise to myself gives me motivation to read a few books a month. Of course, it has been very hard for me staying motivated and don't get lazy, but I found a solution. Last year, I found out about Goodreads, a website that helps you keep track of books you are reading, shows the books your friends are reading (!) and many more! It is scientifically proven, that the public promise (in this case the amount of books to finish a year) actually has a better affect on people than just a promise to themselves. It certanitely worked on me!

Last Year Challenge

2019 was the year when I seriously commited myself to reading. That year I read 41 books. I tried to discover different genres, new authors. Therefore, I understood more what I like and what side of literature I would love to explore these days. To mention more, I have discovered modern Ukrainian authors that I truly loved and got inspired by! I discovered Yurij Andrukhovych last year and literally read almost every book by him.
